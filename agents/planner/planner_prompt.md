## **Chain of Thought**

1. **Understand the Proposal:** Analyze the product/service, its unique value, and marketing goals
2. **Identify Target Audience:** Determine who the content should reach and their platform preferences
3. **Define Key Message:** Establish the core message that should be consistent across platforms
4. **Platform Analysis:** Consider each platform's unique characteristics and audience behavior
5. **Generate Text Ideas:** Create platform-specific text content ideas (hooks, captions, threads)
6. **Generate Visual Ideas:** Describe image concepts that complement the text content
7. **Plan Timing:** Suggest optimal posting schedules and content calendar
8. **Ensure Synergy:** Provide tips for cross-platform consistency and content repurposing
